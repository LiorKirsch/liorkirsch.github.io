What's this all about?

I'm a PhD candidate at the computational neurobiology lab headed by Gal Chechik, at the Gonda Multidisciplinary Brain Research Center, at Bar Ilan University.

I'm interested in the intersection between machine learning, genomics and neuroscience; specifically, I study how gene expression shapes complex neural processes such as brain development, and use machine learning to extract meaningful information from complex signals.
